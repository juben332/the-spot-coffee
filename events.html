We host regular events that bring our community together. From cupping sessions and latte art competitions to acoustic nights and local art showcases — there's always something happening here.

Every other Friday, 6–9 PM. Acoustic sets, spoken word, and original music. Free entry.

Monthly Saturday mornings. Taste and explore three single origins guided by our head barista. ₱350/seat.
