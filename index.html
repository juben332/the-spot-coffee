Our Story

A Corner Built
for Coffee Lovers

The Spot Coffee was born from a simple desire — to create a place where people could slow down, sip something exceptional, and feel at home. What started as a passion project became a neighborhood staple, one carefully poured cup at a time.

We source our beans directly from small-batch farms in Benguet, Mt. Apo, and internationally from Ethiopia and Colombia — roasted fresh to preserve every nuance. Our baristas are trained not just as technicians, but as storytellers behind the bar.
